Luncheon with Provost Prabhat Hajela

Register

Mumbai, India

Pan Asian Restaurant at the ITC Maratha

Sahar Andheri (E) 400 099

1/5/2014

12:30 PM

Registration Questions

Office of Alumni Relations

Event Questions

Susan Haight

haighs@rpi.edu

At this informal luncheon, alumni will have a chance to network while Rensselaer Provost Dr. Prabhat Hajela shares new developments at Rensselaer, ideas on how Rensselaer can continue to help and support alumni in their careers, and how to work together to enhance the Rensselaer brand in India.

We hope you will consider joining us. Please email Susan Haight, in the Office of Alumni Relations, at haighs@rpi.edu by December 19, if you are interested in attending.
